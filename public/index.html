Why Choose IEEE SPS?

The IEEE's first society, the Signal Processing Society is the world’s premier professional society for signal processing scientists and professionals since 1948. Signal processing is the enabling technology for the generation, transformation, and interpretation of information. SPS serves its members through high quality publications, conferences, technical and educational activities, and leadership opportunities. Its goal is to keep members abreast of the latest information and to serve the public at large

From HOD Desk

Dr. Varsha K. Harpale, Faculty Advisor, IEEE SPS Student Chapter, PCCOE Vice-chair, IEEE, SPS Pune Section I am delighted to welcome you all to IEEE SPS Student chapter, E&TC, Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engg. (PCCOE). Since many decades, Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) plays important role in professional growth of students allied with Electrical, Electronics, and Communication, Computer Science Engineering education. Students in engineering institute seeking for Knowledge, skills and right attitude to build their careers distinctively. IEEE SPS Chapter aims to provide platform to the students to satisfy their needs and expectations applying professional ethics. Participating in IEEE events and volunteering for professional activities are the finest ways to avail more opportunities to learn, network with influential people, and get recognition. Objective of the chapter is to encourage the students to organize various events, hackathons, workshops, and guest talks by esteemed individuals to build group leadership and introduce technical updates among the students. To inculcate Signal Processing, AI & ML, Data science I wish the IEEE SPS Student Chapter at E&TC, PCCOE a great success in all its endeavors. I wish all of the office bearers and working team the best of luck in putting their incredible devotion to IEEE’s vision and propelling our student chapter to new heights.
